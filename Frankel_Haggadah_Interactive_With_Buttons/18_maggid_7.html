Maggid – Part 7: Splitting the Sea

At the edge of the water, the people panicked. “Why did you bring us here to die?” they cried. Moses raised his staff — and the sea split. A path through the waters, dry ground beneath. The Israelites walked forward, surrounded by walls of water, not knowing what came next — only that they were moving.
Sometimes the only way through is through. Liberation often looks like stepping forward into the unknown — trusting the path will hold.
