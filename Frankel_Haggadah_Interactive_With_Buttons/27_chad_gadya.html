Chad Gadya – One Little Goat

Chad gadya, chad gadya Di-zabin Aba bitrei zuzey Chad gadya, chad gadya Va’ata shunra v’achla l’gadya Chad gadya... Va’ata kelev v’nashach l’shunra... Va’ata chutra v’hika l’kelev... Va’ata eish v’saraf l’chutra... Va’ata mayim v’chaba l’eish... Va’ata shor v’shata l’maya... Va’ata shochet v’shachat l’shor... Va’ata Malach HaMavet v’shachat l’shochet... Va’ata HaKadosh Baruch Hu v’shachat l’Malach HaMavet...
A nursery rhyme? A parable? A metaphor for divine justice? Whatever it is, Chad Gadya is delightfully chaotic — sung faster and faster, chasing our breath to the end.
