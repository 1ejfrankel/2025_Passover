Adir Hu – The Mighty One

Adir hu, yivneh veito b’karov, Bimhera v’yameinu, b’karov. El b’nei, El b’nei, b’nei veito b’karov! He is mighty, He is mighty, May He soon rebuild His house. Speedily, speedily and in our days, soon. God, rebuild, God, rebuild— Rebuild Your house soon!
A hopeful ending. A prayer for rebuilding — not just a Temple, but a world of justice and joy.
