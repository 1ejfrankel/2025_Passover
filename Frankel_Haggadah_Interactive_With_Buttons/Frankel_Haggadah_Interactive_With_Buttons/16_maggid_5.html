Maggid – Part 5: The Ten Plagues

  • Blood דָּם
  • Frogs צְפַרְדֵּעַ
  • Lice כִּנִּים
  • Wild Beasts עָרוֹב
  • Pestilence דֶּבֶר
  • Boils שְׁחִין
  • Hail בָּרָד
  • Locusts אַרְבֶּה
  • Darkness חֹשֶׁךְ
  • Death of the Firstborn מַכַּת בְּכוֹרוֹת
We remember the pain of Egypt too. As we recite each plague, we dip our finger in wine and lessen our cup — because our joy is never complete when others suffer.
