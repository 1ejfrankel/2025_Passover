Hallel – Songs of Praise

We lift our voices in gratitude and song. Not because the world is perfect, but because we believe it can be better.
הַלְלוּיָהּ
Hallelujah!
These psalms of praise remind us that joy and celebration are part of the work of justice.
We don’t sing because everything is fine. We sing because we believe in freedom, and joy helps us carry the world.
