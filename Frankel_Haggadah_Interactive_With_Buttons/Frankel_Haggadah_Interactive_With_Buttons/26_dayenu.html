Dayenu – It Would Have Been Enough

Ilu hotzi hotzianu, hotzianu mi-Mitzrayim – Dayenu! Ilu asah bahem sh’fatim – Dayenu! Ilu kara lanu et ha-yam – Dayenu! Ilu he’eviranu b’tocho b’yabashah – Dayenu! Ilu shaka b’tzarenu b’tocheha – Dayenu! Ilu tzarchenu bimemotav – Dayenu! Ilu zan et kol tzarchenu bamidbar arba’im shanah – Dayenu! Ilu natan lanu et haShabbat – Dayenu! Ilu kirvanu lifnei Har Sinai – Dayenu! Ilu natan lanu et haTorah – Dayenu! Ilu he’eviranu l’eretz Yisrael – Dayenu!
Dayenu is a gratitude anthem — a reminder that each step of redemption, each kindness, would have been enough on its own. And yet, we were given more.
